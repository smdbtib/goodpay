Comes for the simple: GoodPay

Let's uncomplicate your finances, the GoodPay application has the proposal to organize your finances using Javascript technology. It will assemble and organize your expenses using a fluid and well-developed Bootstrap interface to facilitate your personal cash. The application is simple to use and will serve to organize in a vector of objects your inputs and outputs based on the use of cards and alerts for your easy understanding of the use of your income and expenses
